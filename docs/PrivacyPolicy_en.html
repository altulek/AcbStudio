Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-11-16

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what technical data may be processed when using the application and for what purpose. The app does not collect personal data for marketing, profiling, or advertising. The technical data described below may be automatically processed by the Unity runtime environment.

1. Data Controller

The data controller is the creator of the application. If you have any questions regarding privacy, please contact us at: altulek@gmail.com

2. Data We Collect

The application does not collect personal data manually. However, the Unity engine and related packages may automatically process the following categories of technical data:

Data category Examples Purpose
App performance and activity data Crash logs, app diagnostics, performance metrics To analyze errors and improve app stability
Device or other identifiers Device identifier (may be hashed), device model, OS version, system language Diagnostics and correlation of errors with hardware/software configurations

These data are used exclusively to improve the quality and stability of the app. They are not used to track users for advertising purposes or to build marketing profiles.

3. Data Sharing

Technical data automatically collected by Unity may be transferred to Unity Technologies, Inc. for the purpose of diagnostics and performance analysis. The data are not sold or shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Retention Period / Ephemeral Data

The data are treated as ephemeral and stored only as long as necessary to perform diagnostics and resolve issues.

5. Legal Basis for Processing

Technical data are processed because they are required to ensure the proper functioning of the application, diagnose errors, and improve performance.

6. User Rights

Depending on local regulations, users may have the right to request access, deletion, or restriction of their data. Due to the nature of the processed data (anonymous or pseudonymous), identifying a specific user may not be possible.

7. Data Security

Unity Technologies applies technical and organizational measures to protect data from unauthorized access. The application does not store personal user data on its own servers.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Policy may be updated if the application or the services it uses change. Any significant changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of the document.

9. Contact

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: altulek@gmail.com

Note: This Privacy Policy reflects only the technical data processing performed automatically by Unity (crash logs, diagnostics, device identifiers). If additional features are added to the application in the future (e.g., login, ads, third-party SDKs), this policy should be updated accordingly.
